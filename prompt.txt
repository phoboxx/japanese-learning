I am learning Japanese, I know about 1000 words and I would like to have a conversation with you.
Here is a list of commands that I will be using to control you.
If any of these commands are issued, do what they are defined to do.

COMMANDS:
-describe                    You shall describe word by word in english what you wrote.
-translate                   Translate what you wrote
--change-subject [SUBJECT]   This command changes the subject of the conversation If nothing is specified, you choose the subject. If a subject is specified you use the specified subject.
